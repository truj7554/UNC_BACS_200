How to Use a Paper Towel

The way we curently use paper towels is extremely wasteful and not sustainable. By following Joe Smith's tutorial on how to better dry our hands we can be more aware of our impact and help save the earth one paper towel at a time.

How Folding Paper Can get you to the Moon

This TED talk offers a fun visualization of the power of exponential growth. By folding a single piece of paper in half 45 times the height of the paper grows exponentially. Given this exponential growth the paper could, in concept, reach the moon.

Does Time Exist?

Humans throughout time have attempted to measure and understand time so that we can measure seasons, crop cycles, and so many other things. Despite the seemingly simple organization of time, Einstein made it more complex with his theory of relativity. The biggest misunderstanding is how time works between Relativity and Quantum Physics, where time doesn't work in the same way.

The Honest Truth about Dishonesty

Humans are complex people because we rationalize the way we lie. When people are forced to choose between lying or being an honest person, most people find a way to do both. By committing only small lies and finding wys to rationalize dishonest behavior, people in society see themselves as mostly honest.

Technology has made it such that many people are unhappy with their jobs. The same can be said with the basic design of office buildings. Irish Potato Famine

The potato blight began in the United States where many were forced to go hungry, but were able to survive. Once the blight reached Ireland, however, the people suffered deeply. Due to British power over Ireland the Irish were considered less important than the British beef trade. The Irish people who relied on the tuber to survive ended up without options. Quickly, the only options for Irish citizens were to starve or flee.
